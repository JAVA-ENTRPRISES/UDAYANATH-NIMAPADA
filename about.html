Legacy of Udayanath Public School

Located in the heart of Nimapara, Puri, Udayanath Public School is more than just an educational institution; it is a community dedicated to excellence. Our campus at Rench Sasan provides a peaceful, green environment away from city distractions, perfect for focused learning.

Since our inception, we have integrated the rigorous standards of modern pedagogy with the cultural heritage of Odisha, ensuring our students are both globally competitive and locally grounded.

Our Vision

To be the leading technological and academic hub in the Puri district, nurturing innovators who lead with empathy.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality English medium education with a focus on STEM and character development.
